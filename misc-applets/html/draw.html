The Brachistochrone Problem

The brachistochrone problem asks: "What is the curve of fastest descent between two points for a bead sliding under gravity?"

The solution is a cycloid - the curve traced by a point on the rim of a rolling circle. Mathematically, it minimizes the functional:

\[ T = \int_{A}^{B} \frac{\sqrt{1 + (y')^2}}{\sqrt{2g(y_A - y)}} \, dx \]

where \(T\) is the travel time, \(g\) is gravity, and \(y_A\) is the starting height.
